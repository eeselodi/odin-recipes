Falafel Pita Sandwich

Falafel wrapped with White Pita Bread and filled with Taratour Sauce and Vegetables

Most likely originated in Egypt and it prevalent in the Middle East

Ingredients

  • [12] Frozen Falafel
  • [1/4 a cup] of Tahini
  • [1/4 a cup] of Water
  • [2 Tablespoons] Lemon Juice
  • [2] Minced Garlic Cloves
  • [1/4 of a Teaspoon] of Ground Paprika
  • [6] Whole-Wheat Pita Bread
  • [1] Wedged Tomato
  • [1/2] Peeled and Sliced Cucumber
  • [1] Low-Sodium Sliced Dill Pickle
  • [1/4] Thinly Sliced Small Red Onion
  • [3 Teaspoons] of Harissa (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit (230 degrees Celsius). Place the Falafel on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until heated throug for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. While falafel bake, whisk tahini, water, lemon juice, garlic, and paprika together in a bowl.
  4. Cut about 1 inch from the top of each pita to form a pocket. Add 2 falafel to each pita with equal amounts lettuce, tomato, cucumber, pickle, and red onion.
  5. Drizzle each with about 1 tablespoon tahini sauce and some harissa.
