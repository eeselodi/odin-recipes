Mante Dumplings

Mini Meat Dumplings dipped in yoghurt and can be a versatile dish as it can be served as an appetizer

Inspired by Asian dumplings, they were brought by 13th century Nomadic Turks and originally prevalent in Anatolia, and more recently in many countries in its vicinity

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Water (Add or reduce if necessary)
  • 2 Peeled Onions
  • 1/2 a Pound of Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper (Amount is left to you per your liking)
  • 3 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Minced Garlic
  • 1 8 oz container of Plain Yoghurt
